Stay Healthy is a well-known hospital that provides treatment for different diseases since 2010. It has four different branches in India. The hospital organizes free health check-up camps every month and is well-known for regular meeting of patients with health experts. Expert doctors of the hospital provide information and possible solutions for patient illness along with regular diet plans.
Currently, Stay Healthy hospital has its head office in Kolkata from where doctor appointments are being managed. The head office manages patient appointments with doctors based on the availability of doctors. Patients either visit or call to the hospital to book an appointment.
